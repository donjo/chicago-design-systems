Before the Design System… Research!

Methods, audits and communications that can help shape the design system before any documentation or code is written. What do your users need, and what will be the most beneficial for your specific use-case?

We’ll show you what we did at Cars.com to research use of past tools, interview prospective users, conduct audits of our patterns and competitors, and use that research to create our newly-launched design system!

Winter 2018

Morningstar Design System: A Reflection On the Value We’ve Delivered In Our First Year

Morningstar’s Design System team will join us on Thursday, January 18th to share their journey from several disparate design systems toward a centralized team driving change across the organization. We will learn how Morningstar defined the need for a design system internally and how they delivered on their strategy. The talk will cover:

  • The value that the design system has already brought Morningstar
  • How it has strengthened Morningstar's visual style and UI components across product teams
  • How the team collaborates and drives change across the organization
  • How product teams are adopting the design system
